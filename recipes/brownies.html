Brownie Recipe

Description

TAKE NOTES because this is the best brownie recipe you will ever see. Brownie recipes are everywhere so it must be very difficult picking which to use. After this recipe you would not go close to any other recipe. Let's go!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oreos
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Vegetable Oil

Instructions

  1. Breask 3 eggs into a bowl
  2. Add 1.5 cups of sugar to the eggs
  3. Add a pinch of salt and vanilla extract to the mixture
  4. Whisk thoroughly
  5. Add 1/3 cups of cocoa powder to the mixture
  6. Add 1.5 cups of oil to the mixture
  7. Add 0.7 cups of flour to the mixture
  8. Mix with a hand mixer till combined
  9. Bake for 35 minutes at 200 degrees celsius and allow to cool
  10. Enjoy your brownies
