Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

Have you been looking for the ultimate chocolate cake recipe. Look no further because it is right here. Buckle up because after this recipe you might be addicted.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Breask 3 eggs into a bowl
  2. Add 2 cups of milk into the eggs
  3. Add a tablespooon of sugar to the mixture
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Add 2 cups of milk as well
  6. Last but not the least, add a cup of oil and no more
  7. Mix thoroughly
  8. Add vanilla extract when you're done mixing
  9. After everything is combined, add a cup of flour and mix it thoroughly
  10. Put into a baking dish and bake for an hour at 200 degrees
  11. Allow to cool when done and enjoy!
