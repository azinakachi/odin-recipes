French Toast Recipes

Description

This is the only french toast recipe you would ever need so buckle up and TAKE NOTES

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Breask 3 eggs into a bowl
  2. Add 2 cups of milk into the eggs
  3. Add a tablespooon of sugar to the mixture
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Whisk thoroughly
  6. Dip the bread inside the mixture and fry over low heat
  7. Remove from heat when golden brown
  8. Mix cinnamon and sugar in a separate bowl
  9. Dip the both sides of fried perfection into the mixture
  10. Enjoy your french toast!
